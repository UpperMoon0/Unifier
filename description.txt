Unifier enhances Terraria by rebalancing game progression across multiple mods.

Features:
- Seamless integration of progression systems between vanilla Terraria and popular mods
- Balanced resource distribution through enhanced boss drops
- Reworked equipment progression paths with meaningful upgrade tiers
- Cross-mod crafting recipes that create logical advancement between content packs
- Rebalanced armor sets and accessories for smoother difficulty scaling
- Unified equipment stats to eliminate power gaps between vanilla and modded content
- Enhanced early-game options without disrupting late-game balance

Unifier aims to create a more cohesive experience when playing with multiple mods, ensuring a smooth progression curve from early game through endgame content.
